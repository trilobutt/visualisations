Move the slider from D65 (6500K, daylight) toward Tungsten (2800K). The uncalibrated patches shift warm and orange. Linear calibration (a global channel-wise scale) partially corrects this but introduces new errors due to the non-linearity of the illuminant's effect. The TPS approach applies a minimum-bending-energy warp across the full 3D colour space, achieving a much closer match to the reference values.
